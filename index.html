About me

I’m currently finishing the first year of my Master’s degree in Data Science and Advanced Analytics at NOVA IMS. My background spans project management, training coordination, and team leadership across international environments. Before entering the world of data, I led multicultural teams, organized large-scale student programs, and supported process improvements - all experiences that continue to shape how I approach data-driven work.

As I deepened my understanding of analytics and machine learning, I realized that my strengths in communication, stakeholder alignment, and structured problem-solving are exactly what make data projects succeed beyond just good models. I enjoy bringing clarity to chaos, whether that means cleaning messy datasets or facilitating collaboration between technical and non-technical teams.

I primarily use Python, but I also have experience with SQL, DataBricks, PowerBI, Celonis, and R. Feel free to explore some of my projects in the section below! .
