Big Data Analytics for Retail: Customer Segmentation & Sales Forecasting (2025)

Built with Databricks, Spark, Python, PowerBI

In this project, we explored how large-scale retail data can be turned into actionable business insights. Using a 1 million-row dataset of consumer transactions over three years, we built a cloud-based pipeline on Databricks to segment customers and forecast product-level sales.

For customer segmentation, we applied K-Means Clustering and visualized cluster cohesion using Graph-Based Plots. For forecasting, we compared several models and optimized a Random Forest Regressor, improving accuracy by 10%. Final outputs were visualized in Power BI, including EDA and clustering dashboards. This project strengthened my skills in scalable analytics, predictive modeling, and business-focused insight delivery with Big Data.
