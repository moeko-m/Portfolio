Built with Microsoft Fabric, Power BI, Python, SQL

Grade: First Delivery - 98% / Second Delivery - 89%

As part of a semester-long project in collaboration with BI4ALL, we worked on delivering a complete Business Intelligence solution for a retail company using Microsoft Fabric and Power BI. Our objective was to support operational, tactical, and strategic decision-making through a Self-Service BI environment.

Our responsibilities included designing a star schema dimensional model (5+ dimensions, 3 hierarchies), building ETL pipelines and dataflows to populate a warehouse, developing advanced analytics in notebooks, and producing paginated and Power BI reports. We created complex DAX measures, including time intelligence metrics, and applied best practices in data visualization to clearly communicate results.

This project integrated real business questions and was successfully delivered in all three phases. It was recognized as a well-executed example by the teaching team and BI4ALL professionals.
