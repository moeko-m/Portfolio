This project involved developing AI-driven sales forecasting models for SIEMENS, aimed at predicting 10-month sales for various product groups. Working with both internal historical sales data and 18+ years of macroeconomic indicators, I applied the CRISP-DM framework to design a robust, explainable forecasting solution.

After preprocessing the data with feature engineering, ADF testing, and Granger Causality analysis, we trained multiple models and ultimately selected XGBoost as the most accurate across product groups. We used RMSE and RMSPE as evaluation metrics, as requested by stakeholders. The models were built individually for each sales group, based on their unique seasonality and economic drivers. I also contributed to designing development and maintenance plans for scalable deployment.
