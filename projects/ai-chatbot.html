AI-Powered Chatbot – Fidelidade (2025)

Built with Azure OpenAI + Flowise

As part of a real-world business case, we worked on developing a functional AI-Powered Chatbot Assistant for Fidelidade, Portugal’s leading insurance company. The chatbot, built with Azure OpenAI and Flowise, was designed to support sales agents by providing instant, conversational access to investment product details (Fidelidade Savings and PPR Evoluir), in both Portuguese and English.

The assistant was planed to be integrated into the company’s internal intranet as a floating pop-up across all pages, allowing agents to consult documents and interact with the bot without switching platforms. Our team ingested 19 internal documents and validated the chatbot’s responses using cosine similarity against a reference Q&A file. We contributed to prompt engineering, semantic evaluation, and deployment planning, ensuring the chatbot met both performance and operational goals. We also designed long-term maintenance and retraining plans.
